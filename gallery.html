How to Add Your Artwork

Follow these simple steps to display your work in our gallery:

1

Prepare Your Image

Save your artwork as JPG or PNG (max 5MB, recommended size: 1200x800px)

2

Upload to GitHub

Go to the images/gallery folder and click "Add file" → "Upload files"

3

Update Artwork Data

Edit the data/artworks.json file to add information about your artwork

4

Wait for Update

Your artwork will appear here within a few minutes after uploading
